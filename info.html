Huot & Vallentin
Archive Info

Pascal Huot and Isaac Vallentin collaborate with artists, brands, cultural organizations, and each other.

Beginning as musicians, Huot & Vallentin found themselves spending a growing amount of time conceiving concert posters, album covers, music videos, and band merchandise. Realizing a living could be made in the exploit of these services, the duo formalized their visual practice in 2012. Their work has since been exhibited and awarded internationally; and they regularly give workshops, lectures, and concerts. Huot & Vallentin are based in Ottawa and Montréal, respectively.
